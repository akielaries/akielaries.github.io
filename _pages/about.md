# Background
I am a Flight Software Engineer at Planet Labs working on payload firmware and software
for satellites. I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science
from Northern Arizona University in 2023 and did a some post-graduate coursework in
Electrical Engineering at the University of Colorado Boulder from 2024-2025.

# Some projects...

- [signalmesh](https://akielaries.github.io/signalmesh/) : Digital synthesizer comprised of a few PCBs
- [openGPMP](https://akielaries.github.io/openGPMP/) : mathematics library I made during undergrad
- [gowin_cortex_m1_fw](https://github.com/akielaries/gowin_cortexm1_fw) : example firmware for the Gowin
supplied ARM Cortex M1 soft core IP. Mostly targeting the 60K and 138K Tang FPGAs using:
    - [mega_60k_test](https://github.com/akielaries/mega_60k_test) : APB, AHB, DDR3, ETH, ARM Cortex M1
    - [mega_138k_test](https://github.com/akielaries/mega_138k_test) : APB, AHB, DDR3, SFP+, ETH, ARM Cortex M1
- [multiflex](https://github.com/akielaries/multiflex) : N-pin (max 32 pins) protocol inspired by the FPC
connectors present on Tang Mega 60k and 138k FPGA dev boards

lots more...
